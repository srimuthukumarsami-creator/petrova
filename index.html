The Problem

Students are choosing careers blind

Millions pick majors, pursue degrees, and enter the workforce — only to discover the role isn't what they imagined. Without real exposure, career decisions are based on assumptions, not experience.

🔍

No Real-World Preview

Students lack access to simulated job environments before committing to a career path or degree.

🔀

Wrong Career Choices

Misaligned expectations lead to costly pivots, time loss, and long-term career dissatisfaction.

📉

Industry Unpreparedness

Graduates enter the workforce unprepared, creating a skills gap employers constantly struggle with.

"Students don't know what a job truly feels like — until they're already in it."
How It Works

From role selection to career clarity

PETROVA's workflow is seamless, intelligent, and continuously improving.

01

User Selects a Role

Choose from a curated library of career paths across tech, design, business, and more.

02

System Assigns Tasks

Role-specific simulations are generated and calibrated to your current skill level.

03

User Completes Tasks

Hands-on challenges in a realistic, interactive simulation environment.

04

AI Evaluates Performance

Results scored across logic, speed, creativity, and accuracy in real time.

05

System Suggests Next Steps

Continue the path, deepen skills, or explore better-matched alternative roles.

🎯
Experience First, Choose After
Empowers users to make informed, confident, data-driven career decisions without risk.

Under the Hood

Built to scale

PETROVA's stack is lean, proven, and ready for rapid growth — no heavy hardware required.

How We Build It

  • Frontend: Web & Mobile interface
  • Backend: Python / Node.js
  • AI Engine: Recommendation system
  • Database: User profiles & performance data

Business Model

  • Freemium model (basic free access)
  • Premium simulations & advanced insights
  • Partnerships with companies
  • Integration with learning platforms

Why It's Feasible

  • No heavy hardware required
  • Can be prototyped quickly
  • Uses existing AI & web technologies
  • Proven, scalable infrastructure
